UX Designer, Coding Enthusiast and Passionate Dog Walker

Webdevelopment and UX design, to me, mean total immersion and flow. The more I have learned since I got started, the more I got to love it and the more I wanted to learn. Now, more than ever, good design and communication through online presence impact everyday decision-making. I studied translation, hoping to make an impact through the facilitation of communication. My workday did not permit me to learn and grow as much as I had hoped. Having been creative and enthusiastic about learning and putting my knowledge into practice, I quickly realized I could do more. Software has always been a focal point of interest, web development, up until now, only a side-project. Over the last couple of years I have been inspired on so many occasions to venture into uncharted territory and do more in the field of web development that I could no longer ingore my urge to follow my curiosity. Get in touch here, click here to see my GitHub profile and here for my portfolio.

More than just a web designer

A good website creates an emotional reaction, a visceral response. Good design not only blends in seemlessly, it touches your heart. I walk dogs for my local animal shelter, because their stories touch my heart. Revamping their outdated and malfunctioning website is a way I love to be able to create meaningful and useful designs. I love being able to save people time, money and generally just to everyday things easier to use.
